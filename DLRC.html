Detroit library and recreational center

The DLRC strives to provide books and clubs to build the body and mind of our community.

An issue I found i my community was lack of a library, I didn't want to have to travel far to get books, so i came up with the DLRC containing helpful programs, access to computers, and access to books. My program will give the community access to programs, camps, computers, books, etc. my business has a competitive advantage because it is both a library and a recreational center.
